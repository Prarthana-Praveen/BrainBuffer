## Basic Details

### Project Description
"Mind Over Matter" is a website which aims to reduce the mental stress of the users by providing them with games, puzzles, motivational quotes, breathing exercises and also a doctors consultation.

### The Problem statement
In today's fast-paced world, many people experience mental stress, anxiety, and burnout. There is a need for an accessible platform that provides tools and resources to help people manage their mental well-being.

### The Solution
"Mind Over Matter" is a web-based platform that offers a variety of features to help users relax and de-stress. It includes games, puzzles, motivational quotes, guided breathing exercises, and an option for doctor consultations, making it a one-stop solution for mental wellness.

## Features

List the key features of your project:
- **Games & Puzzles:** Engaging games and puzzles to help users to take a break and refresh their mind.
- **Motivational Quotes:** A collection of quotes to inspire and uplift the user's mood.
- **Breathing Exercises:** Guided breathing exercises to promote relaxation and reduce anxiety.
- **Doctor Consultation:** A feature that allows users to connect with doctors for professional help and guidance.
- **AI Chatbot:** An interactive chatbot to whom the user can talk to and share their feelings.
- **Gratitude Journal:** A digital journal for users to pen down their thoughts and feelings of gratitude.
- **Zen Garden:** A virtual zen garden to help users relax and focus.
